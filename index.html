Common questions

Why did the puzzles reset at midnight?

The day's set is tied to the calendar date. At midnight in your local time zone, a new set arrives and the previous one is no longer playable.

How do I restore the no-ads upgrade on a new device?

Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore purchases. You need to be signed in to the same Apple ID that bought it.
